What is Velvety?

Velvety is a brand specified in the making of sharpeners exclusively for artists. This innovative product makes the funtion on removing the unwanted remains of wood around the graphite and unscathed at the same time. Now you can have a smoother and cleaner line on your sketches, drawings or any type of work.
